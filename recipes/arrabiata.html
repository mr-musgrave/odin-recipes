arrabiata

description

this simple arrabiata sauce is as easy as it gets with spicy, rich, bright, tomato, basil and garlicky flavors - a must have for every cook! toss it with penne or use on pizzas, subs, and more!

ingredients

  • canned whole tomatoes (san marzano)
  • penne noods
  • crushed red pepper flakes
  • olive oil (extra virgin)
  • yellow onion
  • garlic cloves
  • tomato paste
  • basil
  • parmesan reggiano

steps to deliciousness

  1. boil the noods (until al dente, in salted water)

    add three pinches of salt to boiling water, cook penne just until al dente. reserve some of the pasta water before draining.

  2. sauté aromatics & tomato paste

    sauté the onions in olive oil until translucent, then add the garlic & red pepper flakes. sauté one additional minute until garlic is golden. finally, add the tomato paste & cook one minute.

  3. add the tomatoes & basil (with a pinch of salt

  4. simmer the sauce

    cook the sauce, occasionally mashing the tomatoes with wooden spoon until reduced & thickened

  5. garnish & serve

    remove the sauce from heat. stir in the fresh basil, then stir in the cooked noods. garnish with freshly grated parmesan reggiano & enjoy!
